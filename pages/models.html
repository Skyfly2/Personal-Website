3D Modeling

I have been using blender to create 3D models for the better part of 2 years.
Here is a collection of the models that I have created. This is not an exhaustive list.
Many of these models have been used in the development of video games and 3D animations with my friends.


Telegraph

The telegraph is a model based off of a nineteenth century telegraph.

Loom

The loom was designed based upon a typical 18th century loom.

Jetpack

The jetpack is an interpretation of what a hydrogen jetpack may look like in the future.

Microtransistor

The microtransistor is a scaled up version of a microtransistor

Chariot

An egyptian-style chariot

Buggy

An old-style horse and buggy without the horse.

Computer Tower

A computer tower based on the HP desktops we had at our high school.

Computer Monitor

A computer monitor based off of the ones provided to us by our school.

Nuke

A nuclear bomb based on the "fat man" detonated on August 6th, 1945.

Viking Helmet

A stereotypical horned viking helmet.
